Progressive Web Apps, Offline Apps:
- PWA: https://developers.google.com/web/progressive-web-apps/
- Offline Web Apps, Offline-first Web Apps

PWA:
- "reliable, fast, engaging"
- in reality it means:
  - well-optimized,
  - capable of handling offline situations (at least to some extent),
  - faster "installation" via pinning to home screen,
  - native-like experience

Achieved with:
- well-designed architecture,
- caching strategies,
- engaging animations,
- graceful degradation,
- app manifest,
- service workers
